Frequently Asked Questions

How do I generate a podcast episode?

Save at least 3 links to your Fio, then tap the "Generate Episode" button on the Home screen. Generation takes 2-5 minutes depending on the content length.

Why is my episode taking long to generate?

Episode generation involves extracting content, summarizing it with AI, generating a script, converting to audio, and creating cover art. This typically takes 2-5 minutes. If it takes longer than 10 minutes, try generating again.

Are the podcast voices real people?

No. "Alex" and "Sam" are AI-generated synthetic voices powered by Google's text-to-speech technology. They are not real people.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings and tap "Delete Account" in the Danger Zone section. This permanently removes all your data including saved items, episodes, and account information.

Is the AI-generated content accurate?

AI-generated episodes summarize your saved articles but may contain inaccuracies or misinterpretations. Always verify important information by reading the original sources.
